About

What is the Kinematics Calculator? For that matter, what are Kinematics? Kinematics are the study of motion in physics. The Kinematics calculator allows a user to calculate variables such as speed, acceleration, and displacement in one and two dimensions. Using a series of if/else statements, the program first determines which equation applies to the variables the user knows, and then calculates the remaining variables and displays them.
